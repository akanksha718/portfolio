  • Lecture 4

    In a presentation, strong body language enhances communication. Here are some important tips for effective presentations:

    • Maintain eye contact with the audience to build engagement and confidence.
    • Use hand gestures naturally to emphasize key points.
    • Stand with good posture and avoid unnecessary movements.
    • Speak clearly and at a steady pace to ensure understanding.
    • Lastly, use facial expressions to convey enthusiasm and connect with your audience.
